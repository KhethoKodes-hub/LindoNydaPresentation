Five Core Learnings

1. The Power of Legal Literacy

Legal literacy isn't optional - it's a competitive advantage. Understanding contract negotiation, IP protection, and artist rights is critical for creatives to protect their value.

2. Building a Sustainable Creative Career

A creative career isn't just about talent - it's about sustainability. Treat your craft like a business with focus on funding, innovation, and finding your niche to scale.

3. Strategic Positioning: Finding Your Space

Don't try to fit in any space - find or create the space that needs you. This requires deep self-awareness and understanding of market gaps.

Additional Key Insights

4. Taking Ownership of What You Create

Young creatives should own parts of the narrative, brands, and business model. We are not just implementers, but co-creators who can drive ideas, lead innovation, and claim value.

5. The Opportunity in Collaboration

Growth happens at the intersection of roles. Leveraging networks across legal, social, and business domains creates more opportunities to build meaningful projects.

Key Message: "We are creators, not assistants to creation" - Brandscapers Africa
